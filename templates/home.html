About us:

Ezer Mitzion's volunteer management system is used to manage and organize volunteers, volunteer activities with patients, rides for patients and events intended for volunteers. The system records information about volunteers, the various volunteering activities, patients, events, rides, cities across the country, and the areas of volunteering that take place in the organization.
